Read our story

With more than two decades of experience, Assam Gourmet Catering provides comprehensive solutions by fusing the delicacies of Assam with a love of creating unforgettable occasions.

Welcome to Assam Gourmet Catering, where culinary artistry meets exceptional event planning. Established in 2022, we are a premium catering service based in the heart of Guwahati, Assam. Our journey began with a passion for bringing people together over delicious food and creating unforgettable experiences.

From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we have been privileged to serve diverse events across the region, each time with a commitment to quality and attention to detail. Our team of dedicated chefs and event planners work tirelessly to craft menus that celebrate the rich flavors of Assamese cuisine, as well as a variety of global delicacies, ensuring that every event is a unique culinary journey.

But we are more than just a catering service. We are storytellers, using food and decor to weave the narrative of your special day. Whether it's a dream wedding, a corporate event, or a festive celebration, we bring your vision to life with elegance and flair, making every occasion a cherished memory.

At Assam Gourmet Catering, we believe that every event is a canvas, and our job is to paint it with the colors of joy, togetherness, and exceptional taste. Join us on this journey of celebrating life’s beautiful moments, one plate at a time.

Catering Services

Weddings

At Assam Gourmet Catering, we understand that weddings are deeply personal and special moments. That's why our catering wedding planners work closely with you to create a menu that reflects your tastes and traditions, while showcasing the rich culinary heritage of Northeast India. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we ensure each dish is crafted with care and attention to detail.

Our team is dedicated to providing a warm, inviting experience for you and your guests, bringing the flavors of Assam to your wedding day with grace and authenticity. Let us help make your celebration truly unforgettable.

Corporate Events

Assam Gourmet Catering prides itself in bringing a touch of elegance and warmth to your corporate events. Our carefully curated menus celebrate the rich flavors of Northeast India, offering a delightful and authentic culinary experience.

Whether it's an intimate meeting or a large corporate gathering, we tailor our services to match your unique needs. Each dish is thoughtfully prepared and beautifully presented, ensuring a memorable dining experience for your guests. Allow us to assist you in planning a sophisticated event that is both professional and filled with the inviting flavors of Assam.

Social Events

We believe that social events are all about celebrating connections and creating cherished memories. Our team is dedicated to providing a delightful culinary experience that showcases the vibrant flavors of Northeast India. Whether it's a birthday party, anniversary, or family gathering, we craft personalized menus that reflect your tastes and the spirit of your celebration.

With a focus on quality ingredients and attentive service, we ensure that every dish is not only delicious but also a feast for the eyes. Let us bring warmth and authenticity to your next social event, making it truly unforgettable for you and your guests.

Fresh Eats

At Assam Gourmet Catering, we are passionate about bringing you the freshest and most flavorful dishes through our Fresh Eats menu. We believe that good food starts with quality ingredients, so we source local produce and seasonal flavors to create vibrant, healthy meals. Our team crafts each dish with care, focusing on balanced flavors and wholesome ingredients that nourish both body and soul.

Whether you're looking for a light lunch, a delightful snack, or a refreshing addition to your event, our Fresh Eats options are designed to please. Let us bring a burst of freshness and joy to your table, making every bite a celebration of nature’s best.
